The Last Of Us 2

Developers: Naughty Dog

Five years after that dangerous journey through a pandemic-stricken United States, Ellie and Joel put down roots in Jackson, Wyoming. Living in a thriving community of survivors gave them peace and stability despite the constant threat of the infected and other more desperate survivors. When a violent event disrupts the peace, Ellie embarks on a tireless journey in search of justice and closure. As he hunts down those responsible one by one, he must face the devastating physical and emotional consequences of his actions.
A complex and emotional story Experience the growing moral conflicts that Ellie faces in her relentless quest for revenge. The cycle of violence that surrounds her will change your notions of justice, good and evil, and who is the hero and the villain.
A beautiful and at the same time dangerous world Take the journey with Ellie, from the peaceful mountains and forests of Jackson to the vast, green ruins of Seattle. Discover new groups of survivors, treacherous and unknown environments, and the terrifying evolutions of the infected. The latest iteration of the Naughty Dog engine brings to life a world and deadly characters that are far more detailed and realistic than ever before.
Survival and action gameplay, intense and wild New and evolved gameplay systems deliver a realistic version of the life-or-death decisions Ellie makes on her journey through this hostile world. Feel yourself desperately fighting to survive with enhanced high-intensity melee combat, fluid movement, and dynamic camouflage. A wide variety of weapons, crafting items, skills and upgrades allow you to customize Ellie to your play style.
